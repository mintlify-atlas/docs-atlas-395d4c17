Raffi provides powerful library management features to organize and track your movies and TV shows.

## Continue Watching

Automatically track your viewing progress and resume where you left off:

### Progress Tracking

- **Automatic saving** - Progress saved every few seconds during playback
- **Cross-device sync** - Resume on any device when signed in
- **Per-episode tracking** - Each TV episode maintains separate progress
- **Smart resume** - Automatically detects when you've finished watching (90% threshold)

<Info>
Progress is saved both locally and to the cloud, ensuring you never lose your place.
</Info>

### Continue Watching Row

The Continue Watching section appears on your home screen:

- Shows content with active progress
- Displays progress bars for each item
- Automatically advances to next episode for TV shows
- Removes completed content automatically

## Custom Lists

Organize your content into personalized collections:

### Creating Lists

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Lists">
    Click "Lists" in the sidebar navigation.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create New List">
    Click the "New List" button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Name Your List">
    Enter a descriptive name like "Movies to Watch" or "Favorite Comedies".
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Adding Content">
    Browse and add movies or TV shows to your new list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Adding Items to Lists

Add content to your lists in multiple ways:

**From Content Details:**
1. Click on any movie or TV show
2. Click the "Add to List" button
3. Select which list(s) to add it to

**From Search Results:**
1. Search for content
2. Click the menu icon on any result
3. Select "Add to List"

**While Watching:**
1. Open the player menu
2. Click "Add to List"
3. Choose your list

### Managing Lists

#### Renaming Lists

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Lists Page">
    Navigate to the Lists section.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select List">
    Click on the list you want to rename.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Edit Name">
    Click the edit icon next to the list name.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Enter the new name and press Enter or click the save button.
  </Step>
</Steps>

#### Reordering Lists

Change the order of your lists:

1. Navigate to the Lists page
2. Use the up/down arrows next to each list
3. Lists reorder immediately
4. Order is saved automatically

<Tip>
You can create multiple lists to organize content by genre, mood, priority, or any system that works for you.
</Tip>

#### Deleting Lists

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select List">
    Click on the list you want to delete.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Open List Options">
    Click the menu or delete button for the list.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    Confirm you want to delete the list and all its contents.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Deleting a list removes it from your library but doesn't affect the actual content. You can always re-add items to new lists.
</Note>

### List Item Management

#### Removing Items

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open List">
    Navigate to the list containing the item.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Item">
    Click on the item you want to remove.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Remove">
    Click the "Remove from List" button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Confirm the removal when prompted.
  </Step>
</Steps>

#### Drag and Drop Reordering

Reorganize items within a list:

1. Click and hold on an item
2. Drag it to the desired position
3. Release to drop in the new location
4. Order saves automatically

### List Playback

Play through list items sequentially:

1. Open any list
2. Click on an item to start playing
3. Next items in the list will be suggested when current item finishes
4. Continue through your entire list in order

<Info>
List playback respects your custom ordering, playing items in the sequence you arranged.
</Info>

## Search and Discovery

### Searching Content

Raffi provides powerful search across multiple sources:

**Search Sources:**
- **Cinemeta** - Large database of movies and TV shows
- **Addon Catalogs** - Search through your configured addons
- **Combined Results** - Merged results from all sources

### Search Features

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Query">
    Type in the search box on the home screen.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Results">
    Results appear in real-time, split into Movies and Series tabs.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Filter Results">
    Switch between tabs to focus on movies or TV shows.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Content">
    Click any result to view details and available streams.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Search Performance

- **Instant results** from Cinemeta database
- **Addon search timeout** - 1.8 seconds max per addon
- **Cached results** - Recent searches cached for 60 seconds
- **Deduplication** - Duplicate results automatically merged

### Content Metadata

Each search result includes:

- **Title** and **Year**
- **Poster image**
- **IMDb ID**
- **Content type** (Movie or Series)
- **Release information**

## Popular Content

Discover trending content:

### Popular Sections

- **Popular Movies** - Trending films
- **Popular Series** - Trending TV shows
- **Automatically updated** - Fresh recommendations daily

### Browsing Popular Content

1. Navigate to the home screen
2. Scroll to "Popular Movies" or "Popular Series"
3. Click any title to view details
4. Add to lists or start watching immediately

## Local Library

Manage locally stored video files:

### Adding Local Files

1. Click "Local Library" in the sidebar
2. Click "Add Folder" or "Add Files"
3. Browse to your video files
4. Raffi scans and matches content to metadata

### Metadata Matching

Raffi automatically identifies local files:

- Extracts titles from filenames
- Matches against online databases
- Retrieves posters, descriptions, and cast information
- Organizes by detected content

<Tip>
Use clear filenames with year information for best matching results (e.g., "Movie Title (2020).mp4").
</Tip>

## Episode Management

For TV series, Raffi provides comprehensive episode tracking:

### Season Navigation

- View all seasons for any series
- Browse episodes within each season
- See episode titles and descriptions
- Check watched status at a glance

### Episode Tracking

- **Per-episode progress** - Track each episode individually
- **Watched indicators** - Visual markers for completed episodes
- **Next episode suggestions** - Automatic progression to next unwatched episode
- **Season completion** - Track progress through entire seasons

### Binge Watching Features

1. Start any episode
2. When it ends, next episode prompt appears
3. Click to immediately start next episode
4. Or wait for automatic progression (5-second countdown)

<Info>
Raffi remembers your position in each series, making it easy to continue watching from where you left off.
</Info>

## Data Synchronization

### Cloud Sync

When signed in, your library syncs across devices:

**Synced Data:**
- Custom lists and their contents
- Watch progress for all content
- List ordering and organization
- Favorite status
- Addon configuration

**Not Synced:**
- Local file library
- Player settings (volume, subtitle delay)
- UI preferences

### Offline Support

Your library works offline:

- Access previously loaded content metadata
- View your lists and organization
- Sync changes when connection restored
- Local playback continues working

## Advanced Features

### Catalog Browsing

Browse content from addon catalogs:

1. Navigate to catalogs section
2. Select an addon catalog
3. Browse by genre, popularity, or other filters
4. Click any item to view details

### Metadata Caching

Raffi caches content metadata for performance:

- Faster load times for previously viewed content
- Reduced network requests
- Offline access to cached data
- Automatic cache invalidation for freshness

### Multi-source Aggregation

Content information comes from multiple sources:

- **Cinemeta** - Primary metadata source
- **Addon metadata** - Additional information from addons
- **Fallback logic** - Uses best available source for each field

<Note>
For non-IMDb content IDs, Raffi prioritizes addon metadata over Cinemeta.
</Note>
