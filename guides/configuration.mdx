Raffi offers a wide range of settings to personalize your streaming experience. Access settings through the settings icon in the app.

## Playback Settings

Control how content plays automatically and how episodes transition.

### Auto-play Next Episode

Automatically starts the next episode when the current one finishes. Perfect for binge-watching TV shows without manual intervention.

- **Default**: Enabled
- **Location**: Settings > Playback

### Auto-skip Intro

When enabled, Raffi automatically skips detected intro sequences, getting you straight to the content.

- **Default**: Disabled
- **Location**: Settings > Playback

<Note>
Intro detection depends on metadata from your addons. Not all content will have intro markers.
</Note>

## Stream Preferences

Filter and prioritize streams based on quality and source type.

### HD Only Mode

Restricts stream selection to only high-definition sources (720p and above). Lower quality streams will be hidden from the stream list.

- **Default**: Disabled
- **Location**: Settings > Stream Preferences

<Warning>
Enabling HD Only may reduce the number of available streams, especially for older or less popular content.
</Warning>

### Prefer Debrid Streams

Prioritizes premium debrid services (Real-Debrid, AllDebrid, Premiumize) at the top of the stream list. These services typically offer:

- Higher reliability and speeds
- Better availability for popular content
- Cached torrents for instant playback

- **Default**: Enabled
- **Location**: Settings > Stream Preferences

## User Interface

### UI Scale / Zoom Level

Adjust the interface scale to match your screen size and viewing distance.

- **Range**: 0.5x to 2.0x
- **Default**: 1.0x
- **Platform**: Desktop only
- **Location**: Settings > Preferences

### Seek Bar Style

Choose between two seek bar designs:

- **Raffi**: Inverted style with unique visual design
- **Normal**: Traditional progress bar style

- **Default**: Raffi
- **Location**: Settings > Preferences

### Search Bar Position

Configure where the search bar appears on the home screen:

- **Auto**: Dynamically positions based on content
- **Header**: Fixed at the top of the screen
- **Bottom**: Fixed at the bottom of the screen

- **Default**: Auto
- **Platform**: Desktop only
- **Location**: Settings > Preferences

### Hero Source

Select which catalog provides the featured content on your home screen:

- **Cinemeta**: Default metadata provider
- **Trakt Recommendations**: Personalized recommendations (requires Trakt connection)
- **Addon Catalogs**: Any catalog from your installed addons

- **Default**: Cinemeta
- **Platform**: Desktop only
- **Location**: Settings > Preferences

## Privacy & Analytics

Control what data Raffi collects to improve the app.

### Share Anonymous Analytics

Tracks feature usage and stream types without collecting specific titles or URLs. This helps the development team understand:

- Which features are most used
- Where users encounter issues
- Performance metrics

- **Default**: Depends on build configuration
- **Location**: Settings > Privacy & Analytics

<Note>
Raffi never collects personally identifiable streaming data. Your watch history and content preferences stay private.
</Note>

### Session Replay

Records UI interactions to help debug issues. Text inputs are automatically masked for privacy.

- **Requirement**: Anonymous analytics must be enabled
- **Default**: Disabled
- **Location**: Settings > Privacy & Analytics

### Save Watch History

Keeps track of what you've watched, including:

- Viewing progress per episode/movie
- Last watched timestamp
- Watch status (watched/unwatched)

- **Default**: Enabled
- **Location**: Settings > Privacy

<Warning>
Disabling this setting will prevent the "Continue Watching" feature from working and won't sync progress across devices.
</Warning>

## Integrations

### Discord Rich Presence

Displays your current watching activity on your Discord profile, showing:

- Content title (movie or TV show)
- Current episode (for TV shows)
- Playback status

- **Default**: Enabled
- **Platform**: Desktop only
- **Location**: Settings > Preferences
- **Requirement**: Discord must be running

### Trakt Integration

Sync your watch activity to [Trakt.tv](https://trakt.tv) for:

- Cross-platform watch history
- Personalized recommendations
- Statistics and insights
- Social features

**To connect Trakt:**

1. Navigate to Settings > Integrations
2. Click "Connect" next to Trakt
3. Sign in with your Trakt account in the browser
4. Authorize Raffi to access your account

<Note>
You must be signed in with Ave before connecting Trakt. Trakt integration requires configuration in your build.
</Note>

## Local Library

Scan local media files and make them available as streams.

<Note>
Local Library is only available on the desktop version of Raffi.
</Note>

### Adding Folders

1. Navigate to Settings > Local Library
2. Click "Add Folder"
3. Select a directory containing your media files
4. Raffi will scan for recognizable file patterns (e.g., S01E01, S02E03)

### File Naming

For best results, name your files using these patterns:

**TV Shows:**
- `Show.Name.S01E01.mkv`
- `Show.Name.1x01.mkv`
- `Show Name - S01E01 - Episode Title.mp4`

**Movies:**
- `Movie.Name.2024.mkv`
- `Movie Name (2024).mp4`

### Rescanning

Click the "Rescan" button to update the local library index after:

- Adding new files to existing folders
- Renaming files
- Removing files

## Storage Management

### Clear Cache

Clears cached metadata including:

- Movie/TV show posters and backdrops
- Episode information
- Catalog data

This can free up storage space but will require re-downloading metadata when browsing.

- **Location**: Settings > Storage

### Clear Watch History

Permanently removes all watch progress data. This action cannot be undone.

- **Location**: Settings > Storage

<Warning>
Clearing watch history will remove all viewing progress and cannot be recovered. This affects all synced devices.
</Warning>

## Account Settings

### Ave Authentication

Raffi uses [Ave ID](https://aveid.net) for authentication and cross-device synchronization.

**Sign in to enable:**
- Watch history sync across devices
- Custom lists and favorites
- Watch parties
- Trakt integration
- Settings sync

**To sign in:**

1. Click "Continue with Ave" on the login screen or in settings
2. Complete authentication in your browser
3. Return to Raffi to complete the sign-in process

### Local Mode

On desktop, you can use Raffi without signing in (Local Mode), but some features will be unavailable:

- Watch parties
- Cross-device sync
- Trakt integration
- Cloud backup of watch history

## Subtitle Settings

Access subtitle settings during playback by clicking the subtitle button.

### Subtitle Delay

Adjust subtitle timing if they're out of sync with audio:

- Use **+0.25s** / **-0.25s** buttons for fine adjustments
- Range: -30 to +30 seconds
- Click **Reset** to return to 0

### Local Subtitles

Upload your own subtitle files:

1. Click "Upload .srt" in the subtitle menu
2. Select an SRT file from your computer
3. The subtitle will be immediately applied

<Note>
Currently only SRT format is supported for local subtitle uploads.
</Note>

## Cast Settings

Raffi supports casting to compatible devices using Google Cast protocol.

### Casting to Devices

1. Click the cast icon during playback
2. Select your target device (Chromecast, smart TV, etc.)
3. Playback will transfer to the selected device

**Available controls while casting:**
- Play/Pause
- Seek
- Volume adjustment
- Stop casting

<Note>
Cast functionality is only available on desktop. Some stream types may not support casting.
</Note>
